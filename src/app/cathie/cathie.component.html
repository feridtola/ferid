Cathie Wood

Catherine D. Wood is the founder, CEO and CIO of ARK Investment Management LLC, an investment management firm that managed the largest actively managed exchange-traded fund in 2020. Wood was named Best Stock Picker 2020 by Bloomberg News. She has four decades of investment experience in the financial industry. Her focus is exclusively on listed companies that are disruptive in their industry by being innovative through their technology and business model. She looks for companies that bring about change and have an advantage over their competitors.

ARK Investment Management LLC uses an open research process to seek and identify companies that are leading and benefiting from cross-industry innovations such as artificial intelligence, robotics, energy storage, DNA sequencing and blockchain technology.
